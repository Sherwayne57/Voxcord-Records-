<h3><p style="color:purple">Biography of Sherwayne Bester</h3><br>

<small><details><p style="color:black">

  <h3>
<p>Sherwayne Bester was born on 
24th of July 1988 in Mitchell Plain and is the youngest of his 
3 Brothers.1991 he moved with his mother to live in Elsies 
with his Aunt Nancy for 2 years . 
1993 he and his mother June Bester moved to Self-Help Belhar
to live with his Aunt Elizabeth for 3 years. He was separated 
from his 3 brothers until they got a flat in Chestnut Place Belhar 
where he was reunited with his brothers . He also met his
father <br>
<br> 
 Randall Keith Bester at the age of nine for the first time. 
His interest in music spark from a young age
when his brothers Marchell van Rooyen, Eugene van Rooyen 
and Lindsay Bester played cassettes from the Hifi and rapping.
During grade 4 in primary school  he use to battle rap against 
a grade 7 student though his lost most of the battle's he
continued to grow and started writing songs in grade 8. 
His interest kept growing. He got interested in technical 
side which is Studio producing and even in beat making 
throughout high school and started with fruity loops 3.<br>
<br>

He attended every local stage he could find. And met his 
producer Ruben Rynhardt 2012 and produced his songs and 
1st mixtape called Rise above Circumstances. Ruben help 
him as his producer even with live shows like with startstruck
in 2016.<br>

<br> 
In 2016 Sherwayne entered Starstruck and not only grew his stage
presence but he made history being the first rapper to ever 
achieve contestant of the night as a rapper and also
achieved the same contestant of the night the following week 
heended the show as the last rapper in the top 8 and feels
the show was rigged and knew he couldve win in. So he gave a 
weak performance 
and was kick out in the top 8 because he felt it was a waste
of time if he is not gonna win. He still got 3 awards which 
was the most of the evening. And was awarded the best 
dressed,most creative and best performer.<br>
<br>
He organized a show in his community in 2018 on youth day
and was in collaboration with different organization such
 as Belhar outhreach Forum, PREECO, Smiling one, SAPS and 
   Department of Health,Safety and Security and the 
   honourable Minister Dan Plato.
 There where a range of talent of local artists Feauturing 
 Allen the Cargaurd and Local artist in and around the 
 community of Belhar and has been a great success and 
 have plan to do such events often in the future where 
 he can invite the community to showcase their talents.
As for him he believes this is his moment  rightnow. And 
has his debut album  called "Colorful"<br>
<br>
 He did release his first song as a video on YouTube called 
 'I am a champion ft Ruben Rynhardt'afterward 'Duisende Meile'
 then 'Welcome to Cape Town - Sherwayne Bester and his debut
  single is "Kaapstad Laaitie" which he want to release to 
  radio. His next album name 
 is "Heartbreaks & Opmaak" and  "Trap Room" which he will 
 release after his debut album " Colorful". His main goal is 
 to show his community anything is possible.  He will always 
 want to remain humble and unique from other artists and has
  a passion for directing and editing videos as well</p> 
  </details></H3>
